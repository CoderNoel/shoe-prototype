Smart Shoe Interface

Experience your Smart Shoe's floor-projected fitness interface using cursor movements to control tilt.

How to Interact:

Primary Controls:

  • Move cursor to top of screen to tilt shoe
  • Click on interface elements to select them

Additional Controls:

  • T Toggle cursor tracking (enabled by default)
  • V Toggle voice commands
  • ? Show/hide keyboard shortcuts

Backup Keyboard Controls:

  • ← Tilt left foot
  • → Tilt right foot
  • Enter Tilt forward
  • P Pause workout
  • R Resume workout
  • E End workout
  • S Load sample data

The shoe will automatically tilt when hovering over interactive elements. Click to select.
